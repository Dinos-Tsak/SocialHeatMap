A real-time social nightlife map app.

Vgainoume Today helps people discover the best places to go out based on:
- Live crowd levels
- Venue vibe
- Music style
- Social atmosphere
- Dating & social potential

Think of it as Google Maps for nightlife and social life.

## Vision

The goal is to create a mobile-first app where users can:
- See hotspots in real time
- Discover where people are going tonight
- Find places that match their mood
- Improve their social and dating experience

Future features may include:
- Check-ins
- Heatmaps
- Social profiles
- Dating & matching
- Instagram integration (if allowed)
- AI-based venue recommendations

## Tech Stack
